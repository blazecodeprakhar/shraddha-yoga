The Practice
"Your practice starts with one conversation. What's bothering you?"

I don't hand you a fixed curriculum. Every week we start with a real check-in — what's tight, what's loud, what's tired — and shape the session from there.

Some weeks it's slow breath and long holds because your shoulders are locked from a screen. Some weeks it's a stronger flow because you need to feel something move. The practice adjusts. That is the whole point.

Working with me is less like signing up for a class, and more like having a coach who happens to teach yoga and breathwork — someone who remembers what you said last Tuesday.

Common Questions

Before you send that DM.

If your question isn't here, ask it in the message — I read them all myself.

I'm a complete beginner. Is that okay?
Yes — most of the people I coach one-on-one had never done more than a few YouTube classes before we started. There's no baseline you need to meet. We shape the practice around where your body actually is today.
How is this different from a yoga app or a studio class?
Apps and drop-in classes give you the same session as everyone else. Here, every session is shaped around what's happening in your body and your week right now. Nothing pre-recorded, nothing generic.
What do I need at home?
A mat and roughly two metres of clear floor. A cushion or folded blanket helps. Zoom is the only app you'll need — I'll send a recurring link once we start.
Do you take students outside India?
Yes — I currently have students in 18 countries. We'll find a time slot that works for your timezone. Payment is accepted in INR, USD, EUR, and GBP.
How do I cancel or pause?
Message me. No forms, no cancellation windows, no hoops. If life gets in the way, we pause the membership until you're ready to come back.
I have an injury or a chronic condition. Can I still practice?
Usually yes, but I'll always ask you to check with your physician first. Share what's going on in the DM and I'll be honest about whether I'm the right coach for what you need.
